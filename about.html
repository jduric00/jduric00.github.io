About Me

I’m Jovana Duric, originally from Montenegro and now based in the United States after moving here about six years ago. I’m an architecture and interior design student who enjoys working on calm, light-filled spaces that feel grounded in their site.

The projects on this site – including Fossil Haven Wellness Spa, the Hammerstrom Residence Remodel, and a Two-Story SketchUp House – come from studio work and independent explorations that help me practice drawing, modeling, and clear visual storytelling.

Skills

  • SketchUp for massing studies, sections, and basic walkthroughs
  • AutoCAD for floor plans and elevations
  • Rhino for form and 3D exploration
  • Illustrator & InDesign for diagrams and presentation boards
  • Photoshop for collage and drawing touch-ups
  • HTML & CSS for simple portfolio sites like this one
