Project Highlights

This page shares a closer look at a few key projects from my architecture and design work. Each project focuses on concept, spatial experience, and how light, material, and structure work together.

Fossil Haven Wellness Spa

Fossil Haven is a wellness spa concept set into the landscape at Fossil Creek. The project balances a quiet, almost sketch-like interior atmosphere with more technical sectional drawings that show how the spa tucks into the hillside, opens toward the water, and creates different levels for gathering, movement, and rest.

  • Cliffside spa at Fossil Creek
  • Sections cut through multiple levels
  • Focus on calm interiors and framed views

Hammerstrom Residence Remodel

The Hammerstrom Residence project rethinks an existing family home through a gentle addition and interior remodel. The design focuses on improving circulation between living spaces, opening key rooms toward the exterior, and creating a more cohesive material palette for both the interior and exterior. Plans and elevations from this project explore how small shifts in layout and section can make a house feel brighter, more connected, and easier to live in day to day.

Two-Story SketchUp House

This two-story house study looks at a compact, vertical layout with clear circulation, balconies, and simple massing. It served as a way to practice basic structural logic, stair design, and the relationship between indoor rooms and outdoor terraces.

The animation below is a short walkthrough of the SketchUp model, giving a sense of how someone would move through the different levels and spaces.
