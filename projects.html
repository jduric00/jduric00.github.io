Project Highlights

This page shares a closer look at a few key projects from my architecture and design work. Each project focuses on concept, spatial experience, and how light, material, and structure work together.

Fossil Haven Wellness Spa

Fossil Haven is a wellness spa concept set into the landscape at Fossil Creek. The project balances a quiet, almost sketch-like interior atmosphere with more technical sectional drawings that show how the spa tucks into the hillside, opens toward the water, and creates different levels for gathering, movement, and rest.

  • Cliffside spa at Fossil Creek
  • Sections cut through multiple levels
  • Focus on calm interiors and framed views

Hammerstrom Residence Remodel

A remodel and addition exploring circulation, natural light, and small strategic changes that make a home feel more open, connected, and livable.

Two-Story SketchUp House

This two-story house study looks at a compact, vertical layout with clear circulation, balconies, and simple massing. It served as a way to practice structural logic, stair design, and the relationship between indoor rooms and outdoor terraces.
